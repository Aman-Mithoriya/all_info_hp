HPCL Mumbai Refinery is one of India's oldest and most important refineries, located in Mahul, Mumbai. It plays a critical role in producing petroleum products for domestic consumption and export, contributing significantly to HPCL's refining capacity and operations.

HRRL (HPCL Rajasthan Refinery Limited) is a joint venture between HPCL and the Government of Rajasthan, established to build and operate a world-class refinery and petrochemical complex in Barmer, Rajasthan. It aims to boost the industrial development of the region while contributing to India's energy security.
